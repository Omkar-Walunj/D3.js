Write Up

In designing our webpage for visualizing Nintendo's stock data, we carefully considered various design decisions. We chose an area graph representation to effectively convey the historical stock price changes over time, as it allows users to discern trends and patterns easily. We opted for D3.js for its flexibility and interactivity, enabling users to hover over data points for detailed information. We also incorporated smooth transitions to enhance the user experience.

Our development process was a collaborative effort among team members. We spent approximately 80-100 people-hours on this project. The most time-consuming aspects were data preprocessing and integration with D3.js, followed by refining the user interface for a seamless and intuitive experience. We continuously iterated to ensure the graph's clarity and readability, making sure it effectively serves its purpose as a financial data visualization tool.
